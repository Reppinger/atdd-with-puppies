1. Write a scenario which adds one puppy to the shopping cart and verifies the shopping cart correctly displays:
  - The puppy's name
  - The price for the puppy 
  - The cart total

2. Write a scenario which adds two puppies to the shopping cart and verifies the shopping cart correctly displays:
  - Both puppies' names
  - The prices for each puppy
  - The cart total 

Note: Price for the puppy is the price next to the puppy's name.
Note: Do not worry about optional accessories for this exercise.
